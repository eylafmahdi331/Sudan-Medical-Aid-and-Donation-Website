About the Sudanese Red Crescent

Serving Humanity Since 1956

The Sudanese Red Crescent Society (SRCS) is the largest humanitarian organization in Sudan, providing vital medical aid, disaster relief, and community health services to millions of people across the country.

As a member of the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies, we are committed to the fundamental principles of humanity, impartiality, neutrality, independence, voluntary service, unity, and universality.
